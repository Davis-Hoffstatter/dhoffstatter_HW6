My Background

I was born and raised in Moline, Illinois, and graduated from Moline High School in 2022.

During high school, I played baseball competitively, which opened the door to continuing my athletic career at Black Hawk College, a local community college. I earned my associate degree there in the spring of 2024.

Currently, I’m a senior at the University of Iowa, majoring in Business Analytics and Information Systems.

I chose Business Analytics because I love solving problems with data and uncovering patterns that others might miss.

Outside the classroom, I’m a passionate Chicago sports fan, especially when it comes to the Cubs and Bears. And while it might be frowned on in Iowa City, I also cheer for University of Illinois athletics — go Illini!
